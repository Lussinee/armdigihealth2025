About ArmDigiHealth 2025

We are delighted to announce the ArmDigiHealth 2025 Conference, organized by the Armenian Association of Digital Health (AADH), the Research Educational Center of Digital Health, and the Russian-Armenian University.

ArmDigiHealth 2025 will highlight Armenia’s growing role in digital health innovation. Through collaborative efforts between academia, government, and the private sector, we aim to strengthen healthcare systems, support policy and strategy development, and explore cutting-edge applications of Artificial Intelligence (AI) in health.

Engaging & Interactive Program

The conference offers a unique platform for knowledge exchange and networking, bringing together government representatives, academic leaders, innovators, and startups. Participants will gain insights into the latest trends, research, and practical applications of digital health and AI.

Conference Highlights

    Morning Sessions
    • General Assembly (AADH)
    • MDH Program Presentation (students, faculty, and curriculum overview)
    Afternoon Seminar
    • Session 1: Government stakeholders (MoH, NIH, NCDC, SHA)
    • Session 2: AADH Activities Presentation
    • Session 3: Startups & Companies Pitches
    • Session 4: Open Discussion & Networking

MDH Program – Shaping the Next Generation

Our Interdisciplinary, Innovative Master of Digital Health (MDH) program prepares future leaders in digital healthcare and AI applications. This program fosters practical skills, research expertise, and global perspectives to tackle current and future healthcare challenges.
